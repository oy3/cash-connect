Help centre

Sending funds
  • From the dashboard page, click on Transfer.
  • Type the username or email of the beneficiary in the search field and click the search icon button.
  • In the amount field, enter the amount you wish to send to the beneficiary.
  • (Optional) If you want to add a short note to the beneficiary, type it in the reference field.
  • Double-check all the inputs and click Send.
  • You will receive a success or error alert depending on the outcome.
Requesting funds
  • From the dashboard page, click on Transfer.
  • Click on the "Request Payment" button, which will open a modal.
  • In the search field, enter the username or email of the sender and click the search button.
  • In the amount field, enter the amount you want to request.
  • (Optional) If you want to include a short note to the sender, type it in the reference field.
  • Double-check all the inputs and click Request.
  • You will receive a success or error alert depending on the outcome.
Accept or Decline fund request
  • From the dashboard page, click on Transactions.
  • In the "All" transactions tab, you will see a pending row in the table.
  • To accept the money request from another user, click on the "Accept" button.
  • To reject the money request, click on the "Reject" button.
  • If you want to cancel a request that you made to another user, click on the "Cancel" button.
View transaction history
  • From the dashboard page, click on Transactions.
  • You will see a list of all your transaction history including successful, failed, and pending transactions, ordered by date.
  • If you want to search for a specific transaction, use the search field.
  • On the right side of the page, you will find the Account Overview, which displays your account balance, available balance, income, and expenses.
  • To view your transactions sorted into categories, navigate through the "All," "Expenses," and "Income" tabs respectively.
Updating user profile
  • From the dashboard page, click on Settings.
  • Click on the "Personal Info" tab.
  • Type in the details that you want to update.
  • Click on the "Save Changes" button to update your profile details.
  • You will receive a success or error alert depending on the outcome.
Changing password
  • From the dashboard page, click on Settings.
  • Click on the "Password and Security" tab.
  • Enter your old password.
  • Enter your new password of choice. (The new password must be at least 8 characters long.)
  • Enter the new password again in the "Confirm New Password" field. (Both new password and confirm new password must match.)
  • Click on the "Update Password" button.
  • You will receive a success or error alert depending on the outcome.
Checking rates
  • From the dashboard page, click on Settings.
  • Click on the "General" tab.
  • Select the currency of your choice from the list of available currencies.
  • A table will display below, showing the currency name, currency code, and the rate compared to other currencies.
